EBook reader

  • EBook reader is aimed at providing hassel free reading of ebooks on the website. Works best with Chrome.
  • Its free, its easy and its nice! The way it works is:
  • You register with your email id and start reading books.
  • You can post comments on any of the books if you like them or wish to convey something to the site admin. Your comments are only viewable by you and the admin.
  • Obviously, this site is not as well refined as it should be, but you will be happy to know that the work is in progress.
